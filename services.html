Our Services

At مدرسة نعمان بن ثابت (NBS), we offer a wide range of educational and developmental services designed to cater to students of all ages and levels. Our programs are structured to ensure comprehensive growth—spiritual, academic, and personal—while maintaining a strong foundation in Islamic values. Our services include:

1. Quranic Education

  • Complete memorization (Hifz) of the Quran with accurate Tajweed.
  • Tafseer (understanding and interpretation) classes for contextual learning.
  • Recitation training to improve fluency, pronunciation, and rhythm.
  • Advanced Quranic studies for students interested in scholarship and teaching.

2. Islamic Studies

  • Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence) lessons to instill practical religious knowledge.
  • Hadith studies with emphasis on authentic sources and chains of narration.
  • Seerah (biography of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ) lessons to learn moral and historical lessons.
  • Aqeedah (Islamic creed) classes to strengthen faith and understanding of Islamic beliefs.
  • Comparative religion studies to encourage awareness, tolerance, and informed perspectives.

3. Arabic Language Learning

  • Grammar (Nahw and Sarf) classes to develop strong linguistic skills.
  • Reading and writing comprehension for effective communication.
  • Classical Arabic literature to connect students with historical Islamic scholarship.
  • Conversational Arabic sessions for practical usage in everyday contexts.

4. Modern Academic Support

  • Mathematics, science, and English language support integrated alongside religious education.
  • Study skills, critical thinking, and research methodology workshops.
  • Guidance for school or university exams for students balancing madrassa and mainstream education.

5. Character and Personal Development

  • Morality and ethics sessions rooted in Islamic teachings.
  • Leadership and teamwork training through group projects and community activities.
  • Time management, self-discipline, and goal-setting workshops.
  • Counseling and mentorship programs for emotional and spiritual support.

6. Extra-Curricular Programs

  • Public speaking and Quran recitation competitions to boost confidence.
  • Art, calligraphy, and creative writing classes to encourage cultural expression.
  • Community service programs to instill empathy, responsibility, and civic awareness.
  • Seasonal camps and workshops for immersive learning experiences.

7. Family & Community Engagement

  • Parent-teacher sessions to keep families actively involved in their child’s learning journey.
  • Community lectures and seminars on contemporary Islamic issues.
  • Inter-school events promoting collaboration, knowledge sharing, and social responsibility.

At NBS, we are committed to offering a holistic education that nurtures not just the mind but also the heart and character. Each of our services is carefully designed to ensure that students leave with deep knowledge, strong faith, excellent personal skills, and the confidence to contribute positively to society.
